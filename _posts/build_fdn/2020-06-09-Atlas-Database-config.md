Once your Atlas cluster is active, let's configure it.

1. Setup connection security
   1. Click "CONNECT" on the cluster
<img src="/assets/images/Atlas_CONNECT.png" style="border:1px solid black" width="100%" alt="Under SandBox, click connect"><BR><BR>
   1. On this screen, click "Add Your Current IP Address"
<img src="/assets/images/Atlas_Setup_User.png" style="border:1px solid black" width="100%" alt="click, Add your current IP address from given options"><BR><BR>
   1. Add in a Description so you know it's yours later on. Note: if you reconnect from a different location (e.g. home vs. work) you will need to add new IPs before you can connect. Click "Add IP Address".
<img src="/assets/images/Atlas_Whitelist_IP.png" style="border:1px solid black" width="100%" alt="Given your discription in box below Description"><BR><BR>
   1. Add database access. Take note of these credentials on the template under "Atlas Cluster Admin username" and "Atlas Cluster Admin password".
<img src="/assets/images/Atlas_Add_MongoDB_User.png" style="border:1px solid black" width="100%" alt="Type your username and password"><BR><BR>
   1. Click "Close".

Woo hoo!
